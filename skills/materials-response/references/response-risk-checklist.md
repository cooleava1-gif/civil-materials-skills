# Response Risk Checklist

Before finalizing, check:

- Does every reviewer comment have an explicit response?
- Does every response state a manuscript action?
- Are line/page numbers placeholders or verified?
- Are new experiments actually available?
- Are claims softer where evidence is indirect?
- Are citations real and relevant?
- Is the tone respectful even when rebutting?
- Do figures, captions, and text revisions agree?

Flag unresolved issues as:

- `blocking`: likely prevents resubmission,
- `needs-author`: requires user or co-author decision,
- `polish`: wording or organization improvement.
