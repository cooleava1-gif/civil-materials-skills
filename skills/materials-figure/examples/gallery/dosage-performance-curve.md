# Gallery Card: Dosage-Performance Curve

## Figure Intent

Show how a modifier dosage changes bonding strength, viscosity, storage stability, or retained strength, and identify a defensible optimum range.

## Caption Pattern

Dosage-performance relationship of `[material system]` under `[test condition]`. The shaded band marks the preferred dosage range based on `[performance criterion]`.

## Reviewer Risk

Do not call a dosage "optimal" unless the curve includes enough levels, replicates, and at least one engineering constraint such as viscosity, storage stability, or durability.

## Borrowing Note

Use this card when explaining material-design rationale in an introduction, results section, or thesis progress PPT.
