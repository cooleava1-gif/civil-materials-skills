# Data Availability Statements

Draft statements only after checking the actual dataset package.

## Public Or Supplementary Data

Data supporting the findings of this study are provided in the supplementary dataset, including raw experimental records, processed tables, figure-ready data, metadata, and processing notes.

## Request-Only Data

Data supporting the findings of this study are available from the corresponding author upon reasonable request. Access may be limited where industrial, institutional, or confidentiality constraints apply.

## Mixed Availability

The processed data and figure-ready tables are provided in the supplementary dataset. Raw instrument records are available from the corresponding author upon reasonable request due to file-size or project-management constraints.

## Journal Notes

- CCC-style submissions should preserve mechanism-test metadata and sample preparation details.
