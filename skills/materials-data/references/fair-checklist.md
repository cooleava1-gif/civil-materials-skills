# FAIR Checklist For Materials Science Data

## Findable

- Dataset title, topic, authors, date, and contact are recorded.
- Sample IDs and formulation IDs are stable across raw data, processed data, and figures.
- DOI, repository URL, or manuscript supplementary-file location is recorded when available.

## Accessible

- Data availability statement matches the actual files.
- Access restrictions are explicit, such as confidential industrial data or request-only files.
- Raw data, processed data, and figure assets are separated.

## Interoperable

- CSV or other common formats are preferred.
- Units, standards, test conditions, and processing notes are explicit.
- Column names are stable and machine-readable.

## Reusable

- Replicate counts, sample preparation, curing conditions, temperature, humidity, and aging conditions are recorded.
- Derived values include formulas or processing notes.
- Missing conditions are marked as `[needs confirmation]`, not silently omitted.

## Reviewer-Safe Rule

If a claim depends on data not present in the package, the claim is not submission-safe.
