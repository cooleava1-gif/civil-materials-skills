# Civil Materials Citation Rules

General rules across all civil materials domains:

- Prefer primary research and authoritative review articles over generic web summaries.
- Separate mechanism citations from performance citations.
- For CBM/JBE/CSCM manuscripts, emphasize applied material performance and reviewer-safe evidence.
- For CCC manuscripts, emphasize mechanism depth and composite-material logic.
- For RMPD/IJPE manuscripts, emphasize pavement performance, field relevance, test standards.
