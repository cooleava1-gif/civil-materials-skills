# Review Outline Example

## Topic

Waterborne epoxy modified emulsified asphalt for tack coat bonding.

## Outline

1. Engineering role of tack coat bonding.
2. Emulsified asphalt limitations.
3. Waterborne epoxy modification mechanisms.
4. Bonding-performance test methods.
5. Durability and moisture conditioning.
6. Characterization evidence.
7. Standards, field translation, and research agenda.

## Contribution Logic

The review should synthesize how material design, curing behavior, and interface evidence jointly control bonding performance.
