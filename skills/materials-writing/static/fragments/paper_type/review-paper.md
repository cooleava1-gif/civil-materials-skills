# Review Paper

Use this for a small review, mini-review, or thematic literature synthesis.

A strong review paper is not a paper list. It needs thematic logic:

- define the material system and engineering problem,
- build a taxonomy of modification methods, test methods, mechanism evidence, and service conditions,
- identify the knowledge gap that blocks better material design,
- propose a research agenda that a reviewer can recognize as useful.

For a master's-stage mini-review, keep the scope narrow enough to be deep: for example, waterborne epoxy modified emulsified asphalt tack coat bonding and durability rather than all modified asphalt emulsions.
