# Journal Positioning Writing

CBM writing should emphasize practical material value, complete testing, and comparison with existing construction materials.

CCC writing should emphasize composite logic, mechanism depth, and insight beyond property improvement.

RMPD/IJPE/JRE writing should emphasize pavement relevance, service conditions, and construction feasibility.

JBE/CSCM writing should emphasize application context, case relevance, sustainability if quantified, and clear boundary conditions.

Do not use journal fit as decoration. Let the target journal decide which evidence gets foregrounded.
