# Chinese-to-English Patterns

Use these patterns for Chinese materials prose.

| Chinese pattern | Safer English pattern |
|---|---|
| 本研究通过...研究了... | This study investigated ... by ... |
| 结果表明... | The results showed that ... |
| 其原因可能是... | This may be attributed to ... |
| 进一步证明了... | This further supports ... |
| 对工程应用具有重要意义 | This finding has potential engineering relevance under ... |
| 为...提供理论依据 | The results provide evidence for ... |

## Avoid Literal Translation

- Do not translate `具有重要意义` as `has important significance`; use `has potential relevance` or name the specific contribution.
- Do not translate `明显提高` as `significantly improved` unless statistics support significance.
- Do not translate `绿色环保` as `green and environmentally friendly` without LCA or environmental boundary.
- Do not translate `机理` as a confirmed mechanism when the evidence is indirect.

## Asphalt and Emulsion Terms

- `乳化沥青`: emulsified asphalt or asphalt emulsion, choose one and stay consistent.
- `水性环氧`: waterborne epoxy.
- `粘结性能`: bonding performance or bonding strength, depending on the test.
- `储存稳定性`: storage stability.
- `破乳`: demulsification.
