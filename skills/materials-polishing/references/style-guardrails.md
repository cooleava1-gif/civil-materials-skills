# Style Guardrails

Use this file for mechanical and stylistic checks after the main rewrite.

## Academic style

- Prefer cautious, precise prose over conversational confidence.
- Avoid contractions (don't → do not).
- Avoid rhetorical questions in manuscript prose.
- Define abbreviations on first use.
- Keep figure legends concise (≤ 300 words for most journals).

## Articles

- First mention of a singular count noun: `a` or `an`.
- Later mention of the same item: `the`.
- Generic plural: usually no article.
- Abstract nouns used generally: often no article.

Typical repair:

- bad: `The hypoxia induces ...`
- better: `Hypoxia induces ...`

## Numbers and units

- Use numerals for measurements: `25 cm`, `3.2 s`.
- Leave a space between value and unit: `25 cm` not `25cm`.
- Use en dashes for ranges: `10–20%`.
- Spell out numbers that begin a sentence.
- Use SI units; spell out at first use in text.

## Sentence and paragraph checks

- Each sentence: one main proposition.
- Dependent clauses must attach to a main clause.
- No comma splices (two independent clauses joined by only a comma).
- Each paragraph needs a controlling idea and supporting material.
- Avoid sentence fragments introduced by `although` or `whereas`.

## Overclaim checklist

Flag and soften:

| Avoid | Safer replacement |
|---|---|
| `prove` | `show`, `demonstrate` |
| `conclusively` | remove |
| `unprecedented` | `to our knowledge, not previously reported` |
| `best` | `among the highest`, `optimal within the tested range` |
| `superior` | `higher`, `better under the tested conditions` |
| `first` | `to our knowledge, the first` (only if verified) |
| `always` | `under the tested conditions` |
| `never` | `was not observed in this study` |
| `completely` | remove or qualify |
| `excellent` | remove or use measured criterion |
| `significant` | only with statistical test reference |
| `green` / `environmentally friendly` | only with LCA or emission evidence |

## Integrity rules

- Do not invent references.
- Do not alter quantitative values unless correcting an obvious typo.
- Do not upgrade association to causation.
- Do not imply broader generalizability than the study supports.

## AI boundary

Use AI for language control, not for scientific fabrication.

Allowed:

- Grammar and clarity.
- Restructuring and hedging.
- Translation with terminology checking.

Not allowed:

- Fabricated citations or datasets.
- Invented mechanisms presented as fact.
- Unsupported claims of novelty.
