# Example: Claim-Strength Polishing

## Original

The results prove that the waterborne epoxy modified emulsified asphalt has excellent bonding performance and is environmentally friendly. The mechanism was confirmed by the increased pull-off strength.

## Polished

The results show that waterborne epoxy modification improved the bonding performance of emulsified asphalt under the tested conditions. This improvement may be associated with changes in the cured binder structure; however, direct microscopic or chemical evidence is still needed to confirm the proposed mechanism. Environmental benefits should be evaluated within a defined life-cycle or resource-consumption boundary.

## Risk Note

- `prove` was downgraded to `show`.
- `excellent` was replaced by measured performance language.
- `environmentally friendly` was removed because no LCA boundary was provided.
- mechanism confirmation was softened because pull-off strength alone is performance evidence, not direct mechanism evidence.
