# Reviewer-Risk Checklist

Use for pre-submission self-review.

## P1 risks: likely rejection or desk reject

- Journal scope mismatch.
- No clear research gap.
- No control group.
- Claims exceed evidence.
- Manuscript is a test report without mechanism or engineering significance.
- Review article is submitted to a journal that does not currently accept unsolicited reviews.

## P2 risks: major revision

- Dosage levels are arbitrary.
- Missing durability or service-condition tests.
- Mechanism evidence is weak or indirect.
- Statistical reliability is unclear.
- Figures are redundant or low quality.
- Literature comparison is shallow.
- Engineering application is claimed but constructability is not discussed.

## P3 risks: minor revision

- Abstract overstates novelty.
- Highlights are generic.
- Keywords are not searchable.
- Units, abbreviations, and test conditions are inconsistent.
- Conclusion repeats results without limitations.

## Reviewer simulation output

Use this format:

| Severity | Reviewer concern | Why it matters | Fix |

Then add:

- "What can be fixed by writing."
- "What needs additional data."
- "What should be softened or removed."
