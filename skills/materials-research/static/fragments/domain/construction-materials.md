# Domain: General Construction Materials

Use for broad construction materials, repair materials, composites, building envelope materials, masonry, timber/mineral composites, geopolymers, recycled materials, and multi-material systems.

## Core logic

General construction-materials papers need to define:

- Material function: structural, repair, protective, thermal, acoustic, durability, sustainability, or pavement.
- Service environment.
- Design variable and control.
- Test standard or custom test rationale.
- Performance-mechanism-application chain.

## Reviewer risks

- The material is not clearly tied to a construction use case.
- Tests are generic and do not represent service conditions.
- The manuscript reads like material characterization without engineering significance.
- Sustainability is claimed from waste use alone.
- The study lacks a benchmark material.

When the material fits a more specific domain, prefer that domain fragment.
