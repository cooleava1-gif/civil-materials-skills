# Task: Reading Materials Science Papers

Goal: turn a materials paper into usable research notes, not just a summary.

For full-paper reading, Chinese-English translation, figure/table-aware notes, or literature matrices, prefer the companion skill `materials-reader`.

Default output:

- Bibliographic identity: title, journal, year, DOI if available.
- Research question and material system.
- Experimental design: variables, controls, tests, standards if stated.
- Key results: only numbers present in the source.
- Mechanism chain: what evidence supports what explanation.
- Figures and tables: what each one proves.
- Relevance to the user's project.
- Limitations and follow-up ideas.

For Chinese notes, keep technical terms consistent:

- asphalt/bitumen: choose based on journal convention.
- emulsified asphalt: 乳化沥青.
- waterborne epoxy resin: 水性环氧树脂.
- bonding performance: 粘结性能 or 界面粘结性能 depending on context.

Do not collapse full-paper reading into motivational bullet points when the user needs detailed notes.
