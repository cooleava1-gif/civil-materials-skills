# Task: Reviewer Risk Audit

Goal: find submission risks before reviewers do.

Return findings first, ordered by severity:

- Scope fit risk.
- Novelty risk.
- Evidence-chain risk.
- Experimental design risk.
- Mechanism risk.
- Writing or figure clarity risk.
- Engineering applicability risk.

Use severity:

- P1: likely desk reject or major rejection risk.
- P2: likely major revision concern.
- P3: minor revision or clarity issue.

Reviewer questions to simulate:

- What is genuinely new beyond adding another modifier?
- Are controls sufficient?
- Is the optimum dosage justified?
- Are improvements statistically meaningful?
- Is the mechanism directly evidenced or only inferred?
- Does the work connect to construction or pavement practice?
- Are durability and service conditions represented?

Give fixes as concrete manuscript or experiment actions.
