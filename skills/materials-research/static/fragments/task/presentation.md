# Task: Presentation and PPT

Goal: convert a paper or project into a clear materials research talk.

For full PPT outlines, journal-club decks, or group-meeting slide structures, prefer the companion skill `materials-paper2ppt`.

Default Chinese presentation arc:

1. 工程问题: where the material fails in service.
2. 研究对象: material system and modification idea.
3. 实验设计: variables, controls, and test chain.
4. 核心结果: 3-5 evidence points.
5. 机理解释: evidence-supported mechanism, not speculation.
6. 工程意义: what can and cannot be used in practice.
7. 下一步: missing tests and publishable angle.

For journal club:

- Explain why the paper was worth reading.
- Show the evidence chain figure by figure.
- End with what the user can borrow for their own project.

For thesis-group reporting:

- Emphasize feasibility, timeline, and what data are still missing.
