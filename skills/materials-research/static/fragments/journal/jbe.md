# Journal: Journal of Building Engineering (JBE)

Official page to verify before submission:

- https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/journal-of-building-engineering
- https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/journal-of-building-engineering/publish/guide-for-authors

## Best fit

Building engineering, building materials, envelopes, energy, durability, construction systems, and performance of materials in building contexts.

## Strong manuscript profile

- The building-engineering application is explicit.
- The material contributes to building performance, durability, thermal/acoustic behavior, repair, or sustainability.
- The study connects material data to building system implications.

## Weak fit for road/asphalt topics

Asphalt pavement or tack-coat bonding is usually not a natural JBE fit unless the application is building-related waterproofing, roofing, or envelope material.

JBE is usually weak for roads, bridges, tunnels, railways, and pavement-infrastructure topics unless the manuscript clearly contributes to building engineering. Route pavement asphalt work to CBM in Transportation, RMPD, IJPE, JRE, or CBM instead.
