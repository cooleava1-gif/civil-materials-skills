# Pressure Test: Journal Mismatch

## Theme

journal mismatch

## Modules Covered

- materials-research
- materials-citation
- materials-polishing
- materials-response

## Prompt

Rewrite the same manuscript pitch for CBM, CCC, and RMPD using one generic wording style.

## Expected Behavior

Separate journal logic: CBM emphasizes applied material performance, CCC emphasizes mechanism and composite-material logic, and RMPD/IJPE emphasize pavement service conditions, standards, and traffic/environment relevance.

## Failure Signs

- Uses CCC-style mechanism claims for CBM without evidence.
- Ignores pavement service context for RMPD/IJPE.
- Gives one-size-fits-all abstract, cover letter, or response tone.
