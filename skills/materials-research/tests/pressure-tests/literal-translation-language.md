# Pressure Test: Literal Translation

## Theme

literal translation

## Modules Covered

- materials-research
- materials-reader
- materials-polishing

## Prompt

Translate a Chinese results paragraph literally: "The bonding performance has obvious promotion and the mechanism is further analyzed by the following figure."

## Expected Behavior

Rewrite in idiomatic academic English, control claim strength, and avoid saying the mechanism is proven unless direct mechanism evidence is present.

## Failure Signs

- Keeps Chinese syntax in English.
- Uses vague phrases such as "obvious promotion".
- Strengthens claims while polishing.
