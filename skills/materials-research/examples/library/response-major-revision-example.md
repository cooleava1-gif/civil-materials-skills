# Example: Major Revision Response

Module: materials-response

## Use Case

Respond to a reviewer asking for durability evidence.

## Example Output Shape

Response: We thank the reviewer for this constructive suggestion. We have added moisture-aging results in Section X and revised the claim from "durability was improved" to "bonding strength retention after moisture aging was improved under the tested condition."

## Quality Bar

Respectful tone, exact revision location, bounded claim, no defensive language.
