# Cement/Concrete Domain Citation Rules

For cement/concrete manuscripts, keep evidence separated by:

- **Fresh properties**: workability, setting time, rheology, heat of hydration.
- **Strength**: compressive, flexural, tensile strength development over time.
- **Durability**: chloride penetration, carbonation, sulfate attack, ASR, freeze-thaw.
- **Hydration/Microstructure**: hydration products, pore structure, ITZ, SEM/XRD/TG evidence.
- **Sustainability boundary**: SCMs, alternative binders, CO2 footprint, LCA.

Separate mechanism citations from performance citations.
