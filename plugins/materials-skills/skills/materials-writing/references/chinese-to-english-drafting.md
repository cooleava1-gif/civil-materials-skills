# Chinese to English Drafting

Translate the scientific logic, not the surface word order.

Common moves:

- 抗水损害能力 -> moisture resistance or moisture-conditioned retention, depending on data.
- 粘结性能 -> bonding performance or interlayer bond strength.
- 机理分析 -> mechanism discussion, only if evidence is supplied.
- 显著提高 -> increased or improved; use significant only with statistics.

Keep claim strength below the evidence ceiling.
