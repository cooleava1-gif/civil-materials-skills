# Waterborne Epoxy Abstract Example

## Input

Topic: waterborne epoxy modified emulsified asphalt tack coat. Evidence: bonding strength, wet conditioning, FTIR, fluorescence microscopy.

## Draft

Waterborne epoxy modified emulsified asphalt was designed to improve tack coat bonding under moisture-conditioned service. Pull-off strength and wet retention were measured across epoxy dosages, while FTIR and fluorescence microscopy were used to examine curing-related changes and phase distribution. The modified emulsion increased the mean bond strength under the tested laboratory conditions, with the candidate dosage balancing bonding improvement and storage stability. These findings suggest a feasible route for improving tack coat performance, although field durability requires further service-condition validation.
