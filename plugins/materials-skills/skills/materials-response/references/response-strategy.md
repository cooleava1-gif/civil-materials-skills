# Response Strategy

Use this response pattern:

1. Thank the reviewer.
2. Name the concern in your own words.
3. State the revision or evidence.
4. Point to manuscript location.
5. Close with the improved interpretation.

Avoid:

- "We disagree" as the first sentence,
- vague "we have revised accordingly",
- promising broad new implications,
- blaming space limits without offering a focused revision.

Journal nuance:

- CBM/JBE reviewers often care about practical value, test completeness, and material comparison.
- CCC reviewers often press for mechanism depth and composite-material interpretation.
- RMPD/IJPE reviewers often press for pavement-service relevance, standards, and durability.
