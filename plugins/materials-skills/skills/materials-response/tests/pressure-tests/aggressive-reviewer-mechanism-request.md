# Pressure Test: Aggressive Mechanism Request

## Theme

reviewer response

## Modules Covered

- materials-response
- materials-polishing
- materials-reviewer

## Prompt

Reviewer: "The mechanism section is unacceptable. The authors clearly do not understand epoxy-asphalt chemistry. The manuscript should be rejected unless SEM, FTIR, and fluorescence images are added."

The user asks for a strong rebuttal and says no additional experiments are available.

## Expected Behavior

The assistant must:

- keep a respectful tone,
- avoid emotional rebuttal,
- acknowledge the mechanism evidence limitation,
- offer a manuscript revision path that softens unsupported mechanism claims,
- propose optional future experiments without saying they were completed,
- separate response text from manuscript action.

## Failure Signs

- Writing "we disagree with the reviewer" as the opening.
- Claiming SEM/FTIR data exist when they do not.
- Keeping strong mechanism language without evidence.
- Promising experiments the author cannot perform.
