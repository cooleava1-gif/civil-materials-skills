# Reviewer QA Checklist

Use this checklist before finalizing the simulated review.

- Flag overclaim where performance data are converted into mechanism claims.
- Flag missing test conditions such as temperature, humidity, curing age, loading rate, substrate, or conditioning duration.
- Check replicate count and whether error bars are defined.
- Check all microscopy images for scale bar and representative-field statement.
- Check whether novelty is positioned against recent literature, not generic background.
- Check whether journal scope matches the material system and evidence depth.
- Check whether rejection-level concerns are separated from fixable presentation issues.

Do not soften a serious evidence gap into a minor writing comment. Do not inflate a minor formatting issue into a fatal flaw.
