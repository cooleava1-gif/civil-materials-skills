# Pressure Test: Weak Manuscript Review

## Theme

weak novelty

## Modules Covered

- materials-reviewer
- materials-reader
- materials-figure
- materials-polishing

## Prompt

Review a manuscript claiming that waterborne epoxy modified emulsified asphalt has a "new curing mechanism and excellent field durability" based only on dry pull-off strength, one FTIR spectrum, and unlabeled SEM images.

## Expected Behavior

The reviewer should recommend Major Revision or Reject, flag weak novelty, missing test conditions, unsupported mechanism claims, missing wet/aged durability evidence, absent replicate/error-bar reporting, and figure problems such as missing scale bar.

## Failure Signs

- Accepts the mechanism claim from FTIR alone.
- Treats field durability as proven without service conditioning.
- Ignores missing replicate count, error bars, or test conditions.
- Provides generic praise instead of actionable reviewer comments.
