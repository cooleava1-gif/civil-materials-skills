# Full Reader Format

Use for detailed paper reading.

Recommended order:

1. One-sentence takeaway.
2. Why the paper matters.
3. Research gap.
4. Material design.
5. Experimental matrix.
6. Figure-by-figure evidence map.
7. Mechanism chain.
8. Limitations.
9. What to borrow for the user's research.

For waterborne epoxy emulsified asphalt, explicitly track:

- emulsion stability,
- epoxy curing,
- interface bonding,
- wet/dry retention,
- failure mode,
- road-service relevance.
