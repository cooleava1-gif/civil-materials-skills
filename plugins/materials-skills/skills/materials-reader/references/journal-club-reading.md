# Journal Club Reading

Use for group meeting or paper-sharing output.

Slide-ready reading structure:

1. Why this paper was selected.
2. What problem it addresses.
3. Material design and experimental chain.
4. Three key figures.
5. Mechanism and limitations.
6. How it informs the user's project.
7. Discussion questions.

Avoid turning a journal-club reading into a full translation unless requested.
