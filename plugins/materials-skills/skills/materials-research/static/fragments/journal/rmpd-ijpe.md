# Journals: RMPD and IJPE

Official pages to verify before submission:

- Road Materials and Pavement Design: https://www.tandfonline.com/journals/trmp20
- International Journal of Pavement Engineering: https://www.tandfonline.com/journals/gpav20

## Best fit

Road materials, pavement engineering, asphalt mixtures, binders, emulsions, pavement performance, pavement maintenance, design, modelling, and durability.

## Strong manuscript profile

- Pavement engineering problem is central.
- Testing connects material behavior to pavement performance.
- Road-service conditions are represented.
- Practical parameters such as temperature, moisture, ageing, traffic loading, or construction process are considered.

## Manuscript stance

Use pavement-specific language. Avoid framing the work only as a generic polymer-modified material. Show what the material changes in road construction, maintenance, or service life.

## Targeting nuance

- RMPD is often a good fit for material design, laboratory performance, road-material characterization, and practical pavement-material development.
- IJPE is often stronger when the manuscript connects material behavior to pavement engineering, modelling, design, performance prediction, or service implications.
- For emulsified asphalt bonding work, emphasize interlayer behavior, moisture/temperature response, and construction process rather than only modifier chemistry.
