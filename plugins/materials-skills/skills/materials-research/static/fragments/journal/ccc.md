# Journal: Cement and Concrete Composites (CCC)

Official page to verify before submission:

- https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/cement-and-concrete-composites
- https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/cement-and-concrete-composites/publish/guide-for-authors

## Best fit

Cementitious composites, concrete composites, fiber-reinforced systems, durability, microstructure, hydration, and composite mechanisms.

CCC is a mechanism-demanding target. It generally expects the composite nature of the cement/concrete system to be central, not incidental.

## Strong manuscript profile

- Clear composite-material contribution.
- Strong microstructure or mechanism evidence.
- Durability and mechanical behavior are connected.
- The work advances cement/concrete science, not only mix optimization.

## Weak fit for the user's asphalt direction

Waterborne epoxy emulsified asphalt is generally not a CCC main target unless the paper is about cementitious composites or concrete-interface systems. Prefer CBM, CBM in Transportation, RMPD, or IJPE for asphalt-focused work.

## Routing rule

Do not send asphalt-only papers to CCC. Consider CCC only when the work studies cement/concrete composites, cement-asphalt composite systems, concrete repair interfaces, or cementitious materials modified by polymer/emulsion chemistry.
