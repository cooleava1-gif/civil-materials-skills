# Domain: Cement and Concrete

Use for cement, concrete, mortar, UHPC, geopolymer, supplementary cementitious materials, fiber reinforcement, recycled aggregate, hydration, durability, and cement-based composites.

## Core logic

Cement/concrete papers need to connect:

1. Binder/aggregate/admixture design.
2. Fresh properties and workability.
3. Mechanical performance.
4. Durability and transport properties.
5. Hydration, pore structure, interface transition zone, or microstructure.
6. Sustainability or engineering application if claimed.

## Key evidence categories

- Fresh properties: slump, flow, setting, rheology.
- Mechanical properties: compressive, flexural, tensile, elastic modulus, fracture or fatigue where relevant.
- Durability: chloride migration, carbonation, sulfate, freeze-thaw, shrinkage, water absorption, permeability.
- Microstructure: XRD, TG/DTG, SEM, MIP, NMR, FTIR, calorimetry.
- Sustainability: SCM source, replacement level, functional unit, strength-normalized comparison, carbon or waste boundary.

## Reviewer risks

- Strength improvement without workability or durability balance.
- "Green" claims without LCA or clear boundary.
- Mechanism inferred only from compressive strength.
- Missing curing condition details.
- Overclaiming field applicability from lab paste or mortar data.
