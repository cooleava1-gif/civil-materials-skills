# Domain: Sustainability and Durability

Use when the central claim involves low carbon, recycling, waste valorisation, life-cycle thinking, durability, ageing, or service life.

## Sustainability rules

Do not let "uses waste" automatically become "sustainable".

Ask for:

- Functional unit.
- Reference material.
- Processing energy.
- Transport or local availability.
- Replacement level.
- Performance normalization.
- End-of-life or leaching risk if relevant.

## Durability rules

Durability claims need stress conditions:

- water/moisture,
- freeze-thaw,
- ageing/UV/oxidation,
- chemical exposure,
- temperature cycling,
- fatigue or traffic,
- chloride/carbonation/sulfate for cementitious systems.

## Output preference

Use a trade-off table:

| Benefit | Evidence | Cost or risk | Missing validation |

Avoid one-directional narratives where every change is positive.
