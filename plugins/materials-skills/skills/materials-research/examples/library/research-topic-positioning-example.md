# Example: Research Topic Positioning

Module: materials-research

## Use Case

Position a small review or first manuscript on waterborne epoxy modified emulsified asphalt bonding performance for CBM or RMPD.

## Example Output Shape

- Route: research-positioning / asphalt-pavement / cbm.
- Core gap: interface bonding and service-condition durability are often discussed separately.
- Safe angle: connect material design, bonding performance, and bounded mechanism evidence.
- Evidence needed: recent review sources, direct bonding tests, FTIR/SEM/fluorescence/rheology, moisture or aging evidence.

## Quality Bar

No "first study" claim without search evidence. No mechanism claim without mechanism measurements.
