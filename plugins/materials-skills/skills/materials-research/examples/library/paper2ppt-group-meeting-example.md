# Example: Paper To Group-Meeting PPT

Module: materials-paper2ppt

## Use Case

Convert a paper or literature note into a Chinese group-meeting slide outline.

## Example Output Shape

- Slide 1: Paper identity and why it matters to our topic.
- Slide 2: Material system and test matrix.
- Slide 3: Key performance result.
- Slide 4: Mechanism evidence and limits.
- Slide 5: What we can borrow for our own review.

## Quality Bar

Slides must keep source evidence, figure numbers, and limitations visible.
