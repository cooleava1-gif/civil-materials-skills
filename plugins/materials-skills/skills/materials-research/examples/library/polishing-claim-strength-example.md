# Example: Claim-Strength Polishing

Module: materials-polishing

## Use Case

Polish a results sentence without strengthening unsupported claims.

## Example Output Shape

Original: The bonding performance was obviously promoted and the mechanism was proven.

Polished: The modified emulsion showed higher bonding strength under the tested condition, indicating improved interfacial performance. The underlying mechanism requires further support from microstructural or chemical characterization.

## Quality Bar

Improve English while preserving evidence boundaries.
