# Example: Evidence-Chain Reading

Module: materials-reader

## Use Case

Turn one paper into a claim-evidence-mechanism-boundary table for a literature review.

## Example Output Shape

| Claim | Evidence | Mechanism | Boundary | Citation Role |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Modifier improves bonding | Pull-off or shear test | Not proven unless supported | Condition-specific | Performance evidence |
| Curing improves network | FTIR/rheology/fluorescence | Crosslinking inference | Needs direct data | Mechanism evidence |

## Quality Bar

Do not convert figure captions into unsupported conclusions. Keep numbers tied to tables or figures.
